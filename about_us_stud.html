Introduction

SmartLib is a digital Library Management System designed to make book handling, membership tracking, and report generation faster and easier. We help schools, colleges, and organizations manage their libraries efficiently.

Mission & Vision

Mission: Our mission is to simplify library operations by reducing paperwork, saving time, and ensuring accurate book and member records.

Vision: To become the leading platform for smart and digital library solutions, making every library fully automated and user-friendly.
